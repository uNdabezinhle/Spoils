# Spoils – Brand Kit

**Brand Name:** Spoil  
**Domain:** spoil.co.za  
**Tagline:** Spoil them properly.

---

## Brand Positioning

**Core Idea**  
Spoil is a warm, modern South African gift shop that makes it easy and joyful to thoughtfully spoil the people you care about.

**Brand Promise**  
We help you express care through beautiful, well-chosen gifts — delivered with heart.

**Target Audience**  
South Africans who want to send meaningful gifts to family, partners, friends, colleagues, or clients. They value thoughtfulness over generic options.

**Brand Personality**
- Warm & Generous
- Thoughtful & Caring
- Modern & Approachable
- Slightly Indulgent
- Proudly South African

**Tone of Voice**
- Friendly and warm (never cold or corporate)
- Confident but not arrogant
- Encouraging (“Go on, spoil them”)
- Emotional but not overly sentimental

---

## Taglines

**Primary Tagline**  
**Spoil them properly.**

**Alternative Taglines**
- Go on. Spoil them.
- Because they deserve it.
- Spoil someone today.
- Thoughtful gifts, made easy.

---

## Key Messaging

**Short Description (App Store / Homepage)**  
Spoil is South Africa’s modern gift shop. Thoughtfully spoil the people you love with beautiful gifts, delivered with care.

**About Section**  
Spoil makes gifting easy and meaningful. Whether it’s flowers, hampers, experiences or curated gifts, we help you spoil the people who matter most — beautifully and effortlessly.

**Value Propositions**
- Thoughtfully curated gifts
- Easy and fast delivery across South Africa
- Beautiful presentation that feels special
- Makes gifting feel generous and joyful

---

## Visual Direction (Recommended)

**Primary Colour Palette**
- Deep Teal (#0F766E) – Main brand colour (trust + warmth)
- Warm Gold (#C9A227) – Accent (generosity + celebration)
- Soft Cream (#FDF8F3) – Backgrounds
- Charcoal (#1F2937) – Text
- Soft Blush (#F8E7E7) – Secondary accent

**Typography**
- Headings: Playfair Display or similar elegant serif
- Body: Inter or system sans-serif (clean & modern)

**Overall Feel**
- Warm, generous, and modern
- Clean layouts with generous whitespace
- Photography should feel emotional and human (real people receiving gifts, beautiful product shots)

---

## Usage Guidelines (Summary)

- Always use the full name **Spoil** (not just “spoil” in lowercase when referring to the brand).
- Primary tagline: **Spoil them properly.**
- Use warm, generous language in all communication.
- Photography should feel personal and celebratory.
