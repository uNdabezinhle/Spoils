# Spoils – Full Application Description

**App Name:** Spoil  
**Tagline:** Spoil them properly.  
**Domain:** spoil.co.za

---

## Overview

**Spoil** is a modern South African gift shop application that makes it easy and joyful to thoughtfully spoil the people you care about. 

Whether it’s flowers, curated hampers, personalised gifts, or memorable experiences, Spoil helps users send beautiful, meaningful gifts with ease — complete with personal messages, beautiful packaging, and reliable delivery across South Africa.

Inspired by the warmth of South African gifting culture, Spoil combines beautiful product curation with a simple, emotional, and delightful user experience.

---

## The Problem

Sending a gift in South Africa today is often:
- Time-consuming and stressful
- Limited in personalisation options
- Poorly presented (generic packaging)
- Unreliable in delivery timing and communication

People want to show they care, but current options make the process feel transactional rather than special.

---

## Our Solution

**Spoil** turns gifting into a joyful, thoughtful experience. We help users:
- Discover beautiful, curated gifts quickly
- Easily personalise their gifts with messages, photos, or custom ribbons
- Have their gifts beautifully packaged and delivered with care
- Track their orders and feel confident that their gesture will be appreciated

---

## Target Users

- Individuals sending gifts to family, partners, and friends
- People celebrating birthdays, anniversaries, thank-yous, or “just because” moments
- Corporate users sending client or staff gifts
- Anyone who wants to make someone feel special without the hassle

---

## Key Features

### 1. Curated Gift Discovery
- Beautifully presented categories: Flowers, Hampers, Personalised Gifts, Experiences, and more
- Smart recommendations based on occasion and recipient
- High-quality photography and clear descriptions

### 2. Easy Customisation
- Add heartfelt messages
- Upload photos
- Choose ribbon colours and gift wrapping styles
- Preview how the final gift will look

### 3. Thoughtful Gifting Tools
- Occasion-based templates for messages
- Recipient address book
- Delivery date scheduling
- Gift wrapping and premium presentation options

### 4. Seamless Checkout & Delivery
- Secure payments via Paystack
- Real-time order tracking
- Same-day delivery available in major cities
- Clear communication throughout the delivery process

### 5. Account & Reordering
- Save favourite gifts and past orders
- Easy reordering for regular recipients
- Gift history and reminders
